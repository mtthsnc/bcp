# 09 — Loops

The feedback loop that keeps the brand learning. `brand-check` writes its scores and the conflicts it keeps hitting here; humans review the entries and turn the learnings into new **truths/refusals** (in `01`–`03`) and **decisions** (in `08`).

## What goes here
- **Brand Check scores** for sampled outputs (which sections they cited, where they passed or failed)
- **Recurring conflicts** — rules that keep contradicting each other or gaps the agent keeps falling into
- **What worked** — patterns worth promoting into the canonical sections

## How to author
- One file per period or notable run: `example-loop-2026-06.md`. Free-form markdown — no strict frontmatter.
- Always include a **date line** at the top (e.g. `Date: 2026-06-28`).
- Be concrete: cite output ids, scores, and the truth/decision ids involved.

## What happens next
This is the loop's hinge. A recurring conflict here should produce a **proposed refusal or truth** (authored in `01`–`03`) or a **decision** (in `08`). Once promoted, note it here as resolved so the loop closes.
