For competent weekend-and-beyond hikers who research before they buy, **Northwind** makes durable, repairable outdoor gear backed by a lifetime repair program and published failure data — so the gear is never the thing that ends the trip.

**Why:** Our customer is skeptical of marketing and rewards proof. Anchoring on durability, repairability, and radical transparency differentiates us from fashion-led outdoor brands and earns trust we can't buy with claims.
