# 01 — Imprint

The brand's **core identity**: who we are, what we believe, why we exist. The imprint is the most stable layer — it changes rarely and everything else (voice, design, lenses) derives from it.

## What goes here
- **Positioning** — the single sentence that locates us in the market and the mind
- **Mission / purpose** — why the brand exists beyond making money
- **Beliefs / principles** — the convictions that drive decisions

## How to author
- One truth per file. Each file is a single, defensible claim.
- Every file carries the **truth frontmatter contract** (see repo root) with `type: truth`.
- State the claim plainly, then justify it with a `**Why:**` line. The Why is what keeps the truth honest — if you can't explain why, it isn't a truth yet.
- Cite the raw input it was distilled from in `source:`.

## Keep it tight
Resist the urge to write a manifesto. A strong imprint is a handful of sharp truths, not a wall of aspiration.
