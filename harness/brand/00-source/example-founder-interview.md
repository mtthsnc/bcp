# Northwind — founder interview (raw notes)

**Q: Why does Northwind exist?**
- Started after a washed-out trek where rented gear failed in the rain. "Gear shouldn't be the thing that ends the trip."
- Wants durable, repairable outdoor gear for people who actually go outside — not fashion.

**Q: Who is the customer?**
- Weekend-and-beyond hikers, 25–45. Competent, not elite. They research before they buy.
- "They can smell marketing fluff a mile off."

**Q: What makes us different?**
- Lifetime repair program. Every product has a documented repair path.
- We publish failure data. "We'd rather show the seam that ripped than hide it."

**Q: What should we never do?**
- Never fake urgency or fear. No "you'll be unsafe without this."
- Never greenwash. If we can't prove a sustainability claim, we don't make it.

**Q: Tone?**
- "Like a trusted guide, not a hype man." Plainspoken, a little dry, never precious.
