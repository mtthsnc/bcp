Northwind sounds like **a trusted guide, not a hype man**: plainspoken, a little dry, technically credible, never precious. We use short declarative sentences, concrete nouns, and real numbers. We flex tone by context — warmer in onboarding, more clinical in spec sheets — but never abandon the calm, competent baseline.

- **More like:** "This zipper is rated for 10,000 cycles. Here's the test."
- **Less like:** "Unleash your adventure with gear that won't quit!!!"

**Why:** Our audience researches before buying and distrusts marketing voice. A measured, evidence-led tone signals respect for their intelligence and reinforces our positioning on proof over promises.
