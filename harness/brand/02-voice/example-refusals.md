title: What Northwind never says or does
source: founder interview, 2026-06
date: 2026-06-28
status: active

---

Northwind never:

1. **Sells with fear or fake urgency.** No "you'll be unsafe without this," no countdown timers, no "only 2 left" unless it is literally true. We describe what gear does, not what disaster awaits without it.
2. **Greenwashes.** We make no sustainability claim we can't back with documented evidence. If we can't prove it, we don't say it.
3. **Hides failures.** We never quietly delete a bad review or bury failure data. When gear fails, we publish what happened and the fix.

**Why:** Trust is our entire moat. Each of these tactics buys a short-term conversion at the cost of the long-term credibility our positioning depends on. A single caught exaggeration would cost more than it ever earned.
