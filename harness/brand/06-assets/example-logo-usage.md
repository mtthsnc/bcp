The canonical Northwind logo is `../../../assets/logo.svg` (vector, primary). Use it as the default in all contexts unless a rule below applies.

Usage rules:
- **Clear space:** keep empty space equal to the height of the "N" on all four sides. Nothing — text, image edge, or other logo — enters this zone.
- **Minimum size:** 24px tall on screen, 10mm in print. Below this, use the icon-only mark `../../../assets/logo-icon.svg`.
- **Backgrounds:** full-color logo on light backgrounds; the white knockout `../../../assets/logo-white.svg` on dark or photographic backgrounds.

Don'ts:
- Don't recolor, add gradients, or apply drop shadows.
- Don't stretch, rotate, or distort the proportions.
- Don't place the full-color logo on a busy photo or low-contrast field.

**Why:** Consistent clear space, sizing, and contrast keep the mark legible and recognizable everywhere it appears; recoloring or distortion erodes the single visual signal customers learn to trust.
